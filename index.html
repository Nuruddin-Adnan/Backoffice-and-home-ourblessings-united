Our Mission

We are a Community of People from all over the Globe, coming together to be the Hands and Feet of Jesus for our Family and Friends, along with Personal and Global Communities.

We believe Our Purpose is to reach out to every Person possible in need of economic relief and provide the assistance with dignity, respect, and of our own free will with no personal gain expected.

Our Outreach will be Global and Inclusive for All. We will bring Hope, a Confident Expectation, to all those who join our community.

We are committed to the Privacy and Security of Each and Every Community Member.

Our Community will bring a Message of Hope, Faith and Love to ensure each member knows we are the Salt and Light.

We are a Blessing and will remain United. We look forward to our Journey Together.
